## Syntax guide

### Headers

    # This is an <h1> tag
    ## This is an <h2> tag
    ###### This is an <h6> tag

### Emphasis

    *This text will be italic*
    _This will also be italic_

    **This text will be bold**
    __This will also be bold__

    _You **can** combine them_

### Lists

#### Unordered

    * Item 1
    * Item 2
      * Item 2a
      * Item 2b

#### Ordered

    1. Item 1
    1. Item 2
    1. Item 3
       1. Item 3a
       1. Item 3b

#### Not List
    1\. Item 1

### Images

    ![GitHub Logo](/images/logo.png)
    Format: ![Alt Text](url)

### Links

    http://github.com - automatic!
    [GitHub](http://github.com)
